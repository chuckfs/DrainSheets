# Glossary

## Organization

A company using DrainSheets.

Example:

- A-Team Real Estate

---

## Property

A commercial property, project, or prospecting initiative.

Examples:

- 1040 NJ 35
- Luxury Retail Tenant Outreach

---

## Prospect

A business being targeted.

Examples:

- Gucci
- Burberry
- Bond Vet

---

## Contact

A person associated with a prospect.

Examples:

- CEO
- Broker
- Leasing Manager

---

## Document

A file attached to a property or prospect.

Examples:

- Lease
- Site Plan
- Contact Sheet

---

## Note

Internal commentary related to a property or prospect.
